Price

If a customer wants a rough estimate for grooming use the grooming price sheet.

However, with new clients the price will remain $0 until the groomers can access new pet clients in person. Afterwards, the price will save in Gingr for that pet.

Procedure

We request that you drop off your pet between 6:30-9 for grooming. You can pick up at any time before 8pm. We suggest combining daycare with grooming.

We will not accept large dogs after noon due to the time it takes to dry their coat.

Ask what time the owner will drop off and pick-up and make a note of it in the notes section of Gingr.
