Stay

What to expect

  • Daycare is included in boarding
  • There is no additional charge for administering medication or feeding your pet their personal food
  • After 5 nights of boarding your pet will receive a free bath.

If your pet does not play well with others, then he/she can still get 6-8 walks per day. However, we cannot keep dogs that are aggressive towards people and dogs.

We require 3 emergency contacts that can pick up your pet with 24 hours notice and in the event of a hurricane.

Check-in any time before 8pm. We encourage you to bring a small memento from home or toys as well as their food in individually labeled ziplock bags. However, we can provide house food at an additional cost.

If your pet requires medication, it must be provided in its original container.

Check out time is noon on the final day of your reservation. Any checkouts after noon will incur a late checkout fee of $24, which is the price of a ½ day of daycare. We provide bedding, blankets, and bowls for your pets stay.
