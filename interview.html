Requirements Interviews About Pawlins Services

Interviews

Required for

We do require dog interviews for group play to ensure the safest possible environment, but they are welcome to stay and board with us without the interview as long as they are not aggressive towards people.

Not required for

If they board without daycare, they still get 6-8 walks a day and you can add services like one on one fetch or they can play in our splash park.

We can schedule your interview for you now if you have 15 minutes for us to gather some information about your pet or you may schedule an interview through our customer portal on the website. pawlins.com > reservations

If you want to make any changes or add services, all you need is your email address.

It is a $20 charge for the interview as it requires 2 trained staff members but daycare is free.

Each canine influenza vaccination will cost $45, but you get a free day of daycare! The interview plus the vaccination will cost $65, but you will only be required to pay $45 for the booster and you get another free day of daycare. The booster is due 2-4 weeks after the initial shot.

We recommend that you drop off your pet before 9am for interviews and we suggest that you leave them with us for at least 6 hours to give them the best day possible and find the perfect group of friends. If you are picking up later in the day we may be able to provide services such as grooming.

The interview is done in stages. First we will see how your dog interacts with people. Then we introduce them to a calm dog that has been chosen for the task and gradually introduce one dog at a time until a group is formed.
