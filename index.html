Vaccinations

Most of the vaccinations are standard except for canine influenza. We are happy to call your vet for you to confirm that he/she has all of our required vaccinations.

If you are missing any vaccinations our partner, River Road vet is right next door and can provide the vaccinations for you. However, you are welcome to go to your usual vet before the interview.

If your pet does require canine influenza, we offer a free day of daycare if we provide the vaccinations. A booster vaccination is required if it is their first time receiving it.

Each vaccination will cost $45, but you get a free day of daycare! The interview plus the vaccination will cost $65, but you will only be required to pay $45 for the booster and you get another free day of daycare. The booster is due 2-4 weeks after the initial shot.

Canine influenza was introduced in Louisiana in 2015 and although it is not common here, it is highly contagious. About 80% of dogs that are exposed to canine influenza will contract it. Some local veterinary hospitals have included it as a standard vaccination.
