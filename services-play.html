Play

What to expect

Our daycare is modeled after a children’s daycare and includes physical activities, mental activities, and rest/nap time.

Anyone can watch playgroup webcams on our website.

Half days are 6 hours or less. You can combine half days with grooming or vet services.

  • Outdoor play yards with canine grass (no muddy paws)
  • Indoor play yards with hospital grade rubber floors (soft on joints)
  • Separate play areas for petite pups
  • Outdoor water park
  • Enrichment toys and games
  • Noise-reducing walls for better nap time
  • Play equipment
  • A complimentary bath after 5 consecutive days of day care
  • Training such as group sits
