Wellness

RRVH

River Road Veterinary Hospital is our partner and their building is attached to ours. There is always a vet on site. Because of this partnership, you may have your pets annual vaccinations done while at daycare!

Safe and clean environment

Pawlins is a state of the art facility and is built to provide the best care possible.

  • Our canine grass has an irrigation system and will be sanitized every day.
  • All of the enclosures are designed to prevent cross contamination, never allowing fluids to migrate
  • Hospital grade rubber floors that are impervious to odor and bacteria and are gentle on their joints
  • Our splash park is designed so that it can be efficiently and quickly cleaned.
  • We also filter the air with essential lavender oil which smells nice and is also calming for the dogs.
  • We use a central wet/dry cleaning system so that no dirty mops are ever needed
  • We have a fresh air exchange system that operates every 6 minutes.
